# NyxCodex™ Quick Start — Trainee Guide
### NyxCollective LLC · Clinical De-Escalation Simulation

## Before You Begin
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Work email address (for your account)
- A free Google Gemini API key (instructions below)
- About 60 minutes for full training

---

## Step 1: Get Your Free Gemini API Key
1. Go to **https://aistudio.google.com/**
2. Sign in with any Google account
3. Click **Create API Key**
4. Copy the key — it looks like `AIzaSyD8...`
5. Keep it private — don't share or post it anywhere

---

## Step 2: Open the Training Platform
1. Go to: **https://cmc-creator.github.io/NyxCodex/**
2. Click **Sign Up** (first time only)
3. Enter your work email and create a password (8+ characters)
4. Click **Create Account**

> Already have an account? Click **Log In** and enter your credentials.

---

## Step 3: Enter Your API Key
1. After logging in, look for the **API Key** field in the platform header or settings
2. Paste your Gemini API key
3. This enables Prof. Vance AI coaching and scenario scoring

> The key is used only during your session and is never stored.

---

## Step 4: Start Training!

The platform has **32 slides** organized into modules:

**Core Concepts (Slides 1–6)**
- Reaction gap, body language, grounding techniques, verbal de-escalation, environment control

**Diagnosis Library (Slides 7–14)**
- 8 diagnosis-specific modules: Autism, ADHD, Schizophrenia, Bipolar Disorder, PTSD, Depression, Anxiety, BPD
- Each includes triggers, warning signs, and a de-escalation playbook

**Advanced Techniques (Slides 15–20)**
- Micro-expressions, choice architecture, trauma-informed care, restraint alternatives

**Practice & Assessment (Slides 21–32)**
- Interactive Crisis Lab 🎮 — generate scenarios, type your response, get instant AI scoring (A-F)
- Challenge Drill — timed rapid-fire de-escalation decisions
- Clinical Debrief Assistant — describe a real incident, get mentor feedback
- Final quiz, wellness module, PDF certificate

## Troubleshooting

**"API Error" / scenarios won't load**
- Re-enter your Gemini API key (check for extra spaces when pasting)
- Verify quota at https://aistudio.google.com/ (free: 1,500 requests/day)
- Try a different browser or disable extensions

**"Can't log in"**
- Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login screen — a reset email arrives within 1 minute
- Make sure you're at the correct URL: https://cmc-creator.github.io/NyxCodex/

**"Progress is missing"**
- Log in with the same email you used originally
- Progress is tied to your account, not your browser — it will be there

**"Can't download certificate"**
- Disable pop-up blockers for this site
- Try Chrome or Firefox
- Check your Downloads folder — it may have saved silently
